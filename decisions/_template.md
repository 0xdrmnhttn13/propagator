# ADR-NNNN: <short title>

## Context
What problem forced this decision. What constraints were active (latency, team,
existing infra, deadline). Be specific — "we needed X because Y", not "we wanted
to improve things".

## Decision
What you chose. One sentence headline, then 2-3 sentences detail. Be concrete:
"we use X" not "we should consider X".

## Alternatives considered
- **Option A:** rejected because <reason>. (This is the most important section for
  LLM-guided PR adjustments — when a reviewer suggests Option A, the ADR already
  has the rebuttal.)
- **Option B:** rejected because <reason>.

## Consequences
- (+) what got easier
- (-) what got harder / what debt we accepted

Negative consequences are the valuable part. They tell future-you and the LLM
what to watch for. If a PR feedback addresses a negative consequence, the ADR
may need amending.
